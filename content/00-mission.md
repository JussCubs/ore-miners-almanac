# 00. Mission

The `⛏️｜mining` channel exists to turn ORE mining from scattered chat into shared miner memory.

Thep's emerging goal is simple and important: build a structured education and storytelling hub where miners can explain why they mine, how they think, what mistakes they learned from, and what keeps them convicted when individual rounds get noisy.

## The job of this hub

- Preserve miner origin stories before they disappear in chat scrollback.
- Translate veteran wisdom into reusable beginner guidance.
- Separate true mining from short-term gambling.
- Explain uORE patience, claim fees, and why time matters.
- Turn community lore into onboarding material.
- Give miners a place to point new people when the same questions come up again.

## The core thesis

ORE mining is not about winning every round. It is about building long-term exposure to uORE with patience, consistency, and information.

The channel is becoming the social layer for that thesis. This Almanac is the organized version.
