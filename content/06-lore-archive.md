# 06. Lore Archive

The mining channel is not just strategy. It is also ORE history.

## The first motherlode story

Madhatt3r shared that he was mining before the current V3 shape was designed and before motherlode was fully established. He won around 60 ORE from the first motherlode in ORE history.

The story includes a very human detail: he noticed the motherlode hit while putting his kid to bed, and the system broke because it had just shipped. Chad had to fix a bug later so everyone got their piece.

That is the kind of story that disappears unless someone writes it down.

## Lost media problem

Several people noticed that important discussions, links, and old wisdom get buried in chat. Kemosabe, Wiseman, David, Soohoo, Madhatt3r, Fronk, and other names came up as sources of lore worth preserving.

This hub exists because mining culture needs memory.

## The ORE narrative

Community members repeatedly describe ORE as:

- A fairer digital commodity.
- A Solana-native mining experiment.
- A post-BTC evolution of proof-of-work style distribution.
- A game that turns patience into an edge.
- A community where small miners can still feel part of something bigger.
