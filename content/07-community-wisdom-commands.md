# 07. Community Wisdom Commands

Diggy suggested turning great mining advice into bot commands so volunteers can answer common questions without rewriting the same explanation every time.

This is the MonkeDAO-style pattern: preserve answers, make them easy to summon, and turn chat into an onboarding engine.

## Suggested commands

### `!fronk`

Low-and-slow mining ethos. Mine tiny across 25 blocks, collect uORE, check production cost, and do not expect immediate profitability.

### `!lowandslow`

Beginner-safe accumulation strategy: small size, all tiles, long time horizon.

### `!gambling`

Explains that fewer tiles means higher variance. It can be fun, but it is not the same thing as conservative mining.

### `!uore`

Explains unrefined ORE, claim fees, patience, and why long-term holders care about yield.

### `!safe`

Warns about overdeployment and misconfiguration. Start small. Set caps. Understand the numbers before increasing size.

### `!refinore`

Points miners to refinORE and Roberto as the command center for managing, monitoring, and maintaining a mining strategy.

### `!lore`

Links to the origin stories and early motherlode history so new miners understand they are joining a living community, not just clicking a mining button.
